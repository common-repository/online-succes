== Description ==

With Online Succes you convert anonymous website visitors into new sales opportunities, recognize people based on a form submission, follow them up automatically and increase the ROI of your online marketing activities.

[youtube https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lHbpAktsNS4]

You need a free Online Succes account so you can view the companies and contacts that visit your website.
[Create Account](https://app.onlinesucces.nl/signup)

Our plugin sends all pageviews of your website to our servers so we can recognize the companies and contacts visiting your website.

== Installation ==

1. Go to the Plugins Menu in WordPress
1. Search for "Online Succes"
1. Click "Install"
1. Activate the plugin
1. Go to the plugin settings in the left menu in WordPress Admin -> Settings -> Online Succes
1. Add your Account ID and Tracking ID. You can find them in your Online Succes company profile.
1. Click the button 'Save Settings'
